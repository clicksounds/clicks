Upload Guidelines

  • Must not be a sound that already exists ❓ Ensure your click sound is unique and not duplicated from the mod's existing library.
  • Must have minimal background noise ❓ Click sounds should be clear without unwanted sounds in the background (e.g., wind, static, etc.).
  • Must not be too long ❓ Clicks must not being longer than 1 second.
  • Must not be discriminatory ❓ This is about keeping sounds to not be discriminatory in any way!
    For example a sound that just screams the word like "Gay!" is fine.
    However a sound that puts the word "gay" in a negative connotation like "You suck, Gays!" is not!
  • Keep the sounds family friendly ❓ Children use this mod, so make sure sounds do not contain profanity.
  • Must be in .mp3, .wav, or .ogg format ❓ Sound files must be in .mp3, .wav, or .ogg format.
