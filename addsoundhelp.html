Click Sounds Index Guidelines

  • i Ensure your click sound is unique and not duplicated from the mod's existing library. Must not be a sound that already exists
  • i Click sounds should be clear without unwanted sounds in the background (e.g., wind, static, etc.). Must have minimal background noise
  • i Clicks must not be longer than 1 second. Must not be too long
  • i This is about keeping sounds to not be discriminatory in any way! For example a sound that just screams the word "Gay!" is fine. However a sound that puts the word "gay" in a negative connotation like "You suck, Gays!" is not! Must not be discriminatory
  • i The pack description must explain the pack or not exist. Descriptions such as "idk", "[same as pack name]", or random characters may not be accepted. Description must be useful or empty
  • i Children use this mod, so make sure sounds do not contain profanity. Keep the sounds family friendly
  • i To prevent spam, click packs with the 'Useful' type must have at least 3 click sounds. This does not apply to useful click packs that use digital sounds (such as metronome or osu-hit). Useful packs must have at least 3 clicks

Supported audio formats

.mp3, .wav, .ogg
